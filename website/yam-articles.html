Introduction to the German Volume Training

GVT, also known as the 10 sets methods, dates back to mid-70’s and originates from Germany. Because of this, I decided to name my variant the German Volume Training. ... This method was, to the best of Poliquin's knowledge, popularized by Rolf Feser, the German National Weightlifting Coach of that time, and was quite popular in weightlifting circles as a method to gain weight, often causing athletes to move up one full weight class in only 12 weeks.
